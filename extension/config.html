1. Log in to Home HomeAssistant
2. Click your username (bottom-left corner of the screen)
3. Scroll down all the way, to "Long-Lived Access Tokens"
4. Click 'CREATE TOKEN
5. Name it Quickey
6. Copy the key that is shown in the next dialog and paste it here
